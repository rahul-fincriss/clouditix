

# Clouditix — Enterprise Data & AI Consulting Website

## Design System
- **Theme**: Dark premium — deep navy/black background (#0A0E1A), with blue-purple gradient accents
- **Typography**: Clean sans-serif (Inter), strong hierarchy with large hero text
- **Accents**: Subtle glowing gradients, node/network decorative elements, smooth hover animations
- **Style reference**: Palantir/McKinsey enterprise aesthetic

## Pages & Components

### 1. Homepage
- **Hero**: Full-width dark section with animated gradient mesh background, headline "Transforming Data into Decisions. Powering Intelligent Enterprises.", two CTAs
- **Capabilities**: 5 icon cards (Data Strategy, Governance, AI/Analytics, Cloud Platforms, Industry Solutions) with hover glow effects
- **Featured Solutions**: Prominent cards for Financial Crime Platform (with "NEW" badge) and Guest360
- **Value Proposition**: 3-column stats/outcomes section (faster insight, trusted data, AI-ready foundation)
- **Trust Bar**: Client logo placeholders in a faded row
- **Why Act Now**: Urgency section on AI disruption with a CTA for pilot programs

### 2. Solutions Page
- **Financial Crime Platform**: Product-style layout with problem statement, 7 feature cards with icons, architecture highlights diagram, business benefits with metrics (30-50% reduction), and "See Platform in Action" CTA
- **Guest360**: CDP overview with unified profile, identity resolution, AI segmentation, engagement lifecycle sections

### 3. Services Page
- 5 service areas (Data Strategy, Engineering, Governance, AI/ML, Cloud Transformation) as expandable cards with descriptions

### 4. Approach Page
- 3-phase visual timeline/flow: Discover & Align → Design & Build → Scale & Optimize
- Each phase with icon, description, and key activities

### 5. About Us
- Company mission, leadership positioning, outcomes-focused messaging
- Team credibility indicators

### 6. Contact Page
- Clean form (name, email, company, message, interest dropdown)
- "Start Your Transformation Journey" CTA

### Shared Components
- **Navbar**: Sticky dark nav with logo, page links, "Book a Consultation" CTA button
- **Footer**: Links, contact info, copyright
- **Animations**: Fade-in on scroll, hover scale on cards, gradient pulse on hero
- **Decorative**: Abstract node/network SVG patterns as section backgrounds
